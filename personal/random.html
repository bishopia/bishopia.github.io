The Native Trees of Seattle City Parks

Native plants support healthy and functional ecosystems. Given that I and many others don't know our native tree species that well, I've made a map of many of them found across Seattle city parks (North of Yesler Way because of file size constraints).
These data were pulled from Seattle Tree Inventory Map. I have annotated which species are native to the area and plotted them larger, where color denotes taxonomic family. Non-native specimens are denoted by small black dots.
