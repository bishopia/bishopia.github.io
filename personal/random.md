## Mapping Seattle's Native Trees

Native plants support healthy and functional ecosystems. Given that I and many others don't know our native tree species that well,
I've made a map of many of them found across Seattle city parks (North of Yesler Way because of file size constraints). 

These data were pulled from <a href="https://data-seattlecitygis.opendata.arcgis.com/">Seattle GeoData</a>. I have annotated which species are native to the area 
and plotted them larger, where color denotes taxonomic family. Non-native specimens are denoted by small black dots.

Data from the Seattle Open Data portal.
